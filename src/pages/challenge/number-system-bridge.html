How to Play & Question Types

Your Mission!

Answer 10 questions before the timer runs out. Good luck!

  • Format Conversion: Change a number from the US style to the Indian style (or back again).
  • Words to Number: Read the number in words and type it using digits (e.g., 12345).

Challenge Details

Test your speed and accuracy across a variety of number conversion tasks. The timer duration adapts to your skill level.

  • Format Conversion: Convert between US (e.g., 1,234,567) and Indian (e.g., 12,34,567) formats. Commas matter!
  • Words to Number: Convert written numbers like "Twenty Five Million" to digits.
  • Number to Shorthand: Convert a full number to its common shorthand (e.g., 1,500,000 to 1.5M).
  • Shorthand to Shorthand: Convert directly between US and Indian shorthands (e.g., 1.5B to 150Cr).
